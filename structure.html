Lab Structure and Roadmap

Structure

As a team of engineers and technology experts, we help transform these ambitions into real-world solutions.

Projects Roadmap: Responsible AI Lifecycle

We follow responsible, iterative AI project lifecycles that embed governance, ethical oversight, and impact assessment at every stage.

  • Planning & Design: Define objectives, assess risks and societal impact, engage stakeholders, and map to ethical standards. Collaborate with stakeholders to design AI solutions that are inclusive and context-aware
  • Data Collection & Preparation: Gather, clean, and document data; ensure privacy, fairness, and data quality.
  • Model Development: Build and test models with transparency, bias mitigation, and explainability in mind.
  • Validation & Audit: Evaluate performance, fairness, and societal impact; conduct external audits if needed.
  • Deployment & Monitoring: Launch solutions with ongoing monitoring, feedback loops, and redress mechanisms.
  • Impact Assessment: Regularly assess real-world outcomes and societal benefit, and adapt as needed.
  • Retirement: Responsibly decommission models and manage data retention.

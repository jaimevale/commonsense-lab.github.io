Lab Structure and Roadmap

Structure

Our lab is organized to foster collaboration, innovation, and rapid prototyping. We partner with organizations that seek to adopt AI to achieve outcomes benefiting the communities they serve. As a team of engineers and technology experts, we help transform these ambitions into real-world solutions.

We collaborate with socially impactful organizations, research centers, hospitals, financial and commercial enterprises, and other partners. Projects Roadmap: Responsible AI Lifecycle

We follow responsible, iterative AI project lifecycles that embed governance, ethical oversight, and impact assessment at every stage.

Stage Social Impact Focus Activities and Responsible AI Integration
Needs Assessment & Stakeholder Engagement Community-driven goals Engage with community partners to identify social challenges and define project goals.. Transparency, privacy risk mapping
Co-Design & Planning Inclusive solution design Co-Design: Collaborate with stakeholders to design AI solutions that are inclusive and context-aware. Sustainability, accountability, fairness, explainability, data responsibility
Data Collection & Preparation Context-aware data Data quality, privacy, legal compliance
Prototyping & Testing User-centered iteration Rapidly build prototypes, test with users, and refine based on feedback. Explainability, bias mitigation
Deployment & Training Real-world adoption Implement solutions and provide training to ensure effective adoption. Governance, transparency, monitoring, privacy safeguards
Impact Evaluation & Continuous Improvement Societal benefit measured Impact Evaluation: Measure societal benefits, equity outcomes, and unintended consequences. Use feedback and evaluation to iterate, improve, and scale successful initiatives. Continuous improvement, open reporting
Retirement or Scaling Responsible exit or growth Ongoing compliance, lessons learned

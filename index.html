Mission Statement

CommonSense is dedicated to advancing the creation of IA products with significant social impact, focusing on real-world use cases and product-oriented research that delivers concrete, simple, and tangible results to organizations and individuals. We recognize that collaboration with organizations sharing similar goals is essential to achieving real outcomes. Our core focus is on software engineering, aiming to build maintainable, secure, and high-quality products.

Ethics & Governance

Ensure clear accountability to humans in the loop for all AI-driven processes, maintain a code of ethics with ongoing participatory review, safeguard data privacy, mitigate bias, and engage an ethics board with representatives from key partner organizations.

Open Collaboration

We proactively collaborate with other research labs, universities, hospitals, tech companies, and communities focused on AI governance and ethics. We foster synergistic relationships with stakeholders and end-users to co-create, validate, and scale impactful AI products.
